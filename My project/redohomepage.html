Why These Are My Favorite

The reason why I like these songs is because they remind of "My Nightmare", I like to listen to these songs a lot. These songs really do hit in the feels. All these four songs talks about being with her and how much you love her and no one will feel the way you would feel about her. My favorite line from "Never Ever Land" is " I'ma tell you I feel when the time is right". My favorite line from "Moonlight" is "I just wanna be with youI just wanna be with you tonight". My favorite line from "Til The End of Time" is "I Love You (13x). My favorite line from "So Alive" is "Ain't nobody gon' feel this way, I don't think anybody gon' love you this way".
